I. Series Concept

"In the heart of Taipei’s neon-lit consumerist mecca, the things we discard don’t just disappear; they grow teeth."

殘執 (Residue) is a psychological thriller set against the backdrop of a distorted, modern Taipei. The story explores the concept of "Residual Attachments"—monsters formed from the collective negative energy of urban life: consumerist greed, social media envy, and the existential dread of the working class.

Follow Lin Mo (林默), a man physically and spiritually bound by the "Chains of Judgment." Every judgment he passes adds weight to his own chains, forcing him to confront the thin line between the monsters he hunts and the man he is becoming.
